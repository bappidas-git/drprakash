Meet Your Doctor

Dr Prakash Kumar

Consultant Orthopaedic & Joint Replacement Surgeon

Dr Prakash Kumar is one of Ranchi's most respected orthopedic surgeons, known for combining precise surgical skill with genuine compassion. Patients across Jharkhand travel to his clinic in Kadru for accurate diagnosis, transparent advice and treatments tailored to get them back on their feet — quickly and safely.

Hip Replacement
